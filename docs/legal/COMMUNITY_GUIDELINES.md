# Nepal Kings Community Guidelines

Version: 2026-07-20

Nepal Kings should remain playable, fair, and welcoming.

Do not use usernames, chat, kingdom names, or other player-visible text for harassment, threats, hate speech, sexual content involving minors, doxxing, impersonation, spam, scams, malware links, illegal activity, or attempts to evade moderation.

Do not exploit bugs, automate abusive traffic, attack the server, reveal hidden game state through unauthorized means, or interfere with other players' matches.

Reports may lead to content removal, account restrictions, match correction, or suspension.

Use **Settings → Safety** to report or block another
player. Reports are reviewed in context. Blocking hides direct contact and
prevents new direct messages or challenges in either direction.
