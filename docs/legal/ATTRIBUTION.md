## Audio

Most sound effects and the fallback music are procedurally generated from
project-owned recipes in `scripts/assets/generate_sfx.py`.

Selected spell, card-handling, booster, construction, progression, reward, and
battle-outcome cues are derived edits of 23 Mixkit sound effects. They are used
under the Mixkit Sound Effects Free License, which permits commercial projects
and does not require attribution. Exact source titles, Mixkit item IDs, runtime
uses, and SHA-256 checksums are recorded in
`scripts/assets/audio_sources/mixkit/README.md`.

The menu, kingdom, and battle themes were generated by the project creator
with Suno on 2026-07-13 while the creator held an active Suno Pro subscription.
Their Suno asset IDs are `f3f9f88c-d579-4141-84ea-a9dc67c0aa08`,
`a0e07e8c-1d6d-4680-8d00-ff3a352623c4`, and
`db9a709b-4806-4f4f-a04c-adb5340fd7ae`, respectively. The shipped files are
derived, crossfaded runtime edits authorized for commercial game use under the
subscription terms in effect at generation time.
