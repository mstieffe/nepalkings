# Nepal Kings Support

Nepal Kings is an independently operated game in public-beta preparation.

## Gameplay and account help

Open a support issue at:

<https://github.com/mstieffe/nepalkings/issues/new>

Include the client type (browser, macOS, Windows, or Linux), what you were
doing, what happened, and any support request ID shown by the game. Do not post
passwords, authentication tokens, email addresses, database URLs, or private
messages in a public issue.

## Player reports and blocking

While logged in, open **Settings → Safety**. Player reports
are stored privately for operator review. Blocking a player hides their direct
contact and prevents new direct messages or challenges in either direction.

Do not copy sensitive personal information into a report. For an urgent threat
to someone's physical safety, contact the appropriate local emergency service
rather than relying only on a game report.

## Appeals

Open a support issue with the subject `Moderation appeal` and only the account
username and approximate action date. Do not repost the reported content in
public. The operator can request further details through an appropriate private
channel.

## Security issues

Follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Do not publish exploit details, credentials,
or another player's data in a public issue.
